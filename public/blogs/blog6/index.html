Biography

Born and raised in Dakar, the capital of Senegal,the most western country in Africa and home of the pink lake (pictured above from Dakarlives’ Instagram page). I moved to Montreal, Canada in 2015 to pursue my undergrad in Business Administration with a focus in Finance at HEC Montreal.

While at HEC, I had the opportunity to live for sixth months in Glasgow as part of an International Exchange Program. I loved the experience as it enabled me to discover new cultures, travel and more importantly visit London, the city of my heart.

I’ve long been interested in working in the financial industry therefore, in summer 2017, I interned at Diamond Bank, a commercial bank in Senegal. However, being always open to new experiences, I decided to intern in a Canadian Management Consulting firm CowaterSogema,during summer 2018. The latter works on development projects in emerging countries and supports governments with their finances.After graduating in 2019, I worked at BNP Paribas as a Due Diligence Analyst but decided to join LBS in order to strengthen my candidature for Investment Banking Analyst positions.

At LBS, besides achieving my professional goals, I am very keen on making a long-term impact by contributing to the student community through my ExCo position in the Black in Business Club and working to increase diversity. Outside of university, I enjoy art (music, photography, visual art), traveling, coding (even if it is one of the most challenging experience I’ve had to go through).
